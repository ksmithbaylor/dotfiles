View Advanced Connection Details

The Connections pane shows basic information about a connection. You can also view advanced connection details to see detailed information about a selected connection.

To view advanced connection details:

Option 1

  1. Click the connection to select it.
  2. On the menu bar, click File > Connections > Advanced Connection Details.

Option 2

  1. Right-click on the Pulse connection to display the pop-up menu.
  2. Click Advanced Connection Details.
