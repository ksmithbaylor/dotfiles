Forget Saved Settings

When you connect to a network, you can check the Save Settings check box to have Junos Pulse remember your login credentials. (Note that your network administrator can disable this feature.) Each different screen where you are prompted for a response has its own Save Settings check box. After you save settings, you are not prompted to provide that information on subsequent login attempts. However, if your login credentials change, you must first clear the saved settings in Junos Pulse before you can save the new credentials. Forget Saved Settings affects all saved settings, not just the settings of the selected connection.

If you select Forget Saved Settings while a connection is in a Suspended state, the connection is disconnected.

The Suspend connection option is not available if you choose Forget Saved Settings while you have an active connection.

To clear saved login credentials:

  1. On the menu bar, click File > Connections > Forget Saved Settings.
