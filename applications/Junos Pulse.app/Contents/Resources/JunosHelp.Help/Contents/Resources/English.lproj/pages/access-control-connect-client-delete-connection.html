Delete a Connection

Option 1

To delete a connection:

  1. Click the connection to select it.
  2. Click the Delete button:

  3. You are prompted to verify your decision before the connection is deleted.

Option 2

  1. In the Pulse window, click the connection you want to delete.
  2. On the menu bar, click File > Connections > Delete.
  3. You are prompted to verify your decision before the connection is deleted.
