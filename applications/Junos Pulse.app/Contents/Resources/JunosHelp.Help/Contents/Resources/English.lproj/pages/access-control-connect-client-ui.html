Junos Pulse Interface

The Junos Pulse interface shows your network connections and provides status about your endpoint's connectivity.

The Junos Pulse Connections pane displays connection task buttons, lists your network connections, and lets you connect and disconnect. Click the arrow next to the connection name to expand the connection display and show detailed status. Typically, your network administrator will define your connections but your network administrator can also enable you to define your own connections.

Connections pane buttons:

 Add a new connection.
 Edit a selected inactive connection.
 Delete the selected inactive connection.

Junos Pulse also displays a menu bar icon that provides connection status and enables quick access to connect and disconnect tasks. One menu bar icon provides status for all active connections.
