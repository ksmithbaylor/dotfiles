Extend a Connection Session

Your network administrator can define session timeout limits for your network connections. Your administrator can define a maximum session limit and a session idle limit. Pulse displays a message when you are reaching a limit. The message shows the amount of time left in your network session before Pulse disconnects. If you take no action, Pulse disconnects when the session counter reaches zero. If you click Extend, Pulse resets the timer to its maximum value as defined by the network administrator and continues the session without service interruption.

You can also extend a session without waiting for Pulse to notify you of an expiring session. You can extend a session at any time to reset the session and idle timers to their maximum values. To extend a session:

  1. In the Pulse Connections list, click the connection you want to extend to select it.
  2. In the Pulse menu, click File > Connections > Extend Session.

Or

  1. In the menu bar, click the Pulse icon to display the pop-up menu.
  2. Click the active connection and then click Extend Session.
