Start or Stop Junos Pulse

To start Pulse if it is not running:

  1. Click the Pulse icon on the icon bar, and then click Open Junos Pulse.

    2. If Pulse is already running, clicking Open Junos Pulse opens the Pulse window.

To exit Pulse and disconnect any active connections:

  1. Click the Pulse icon on the icon bar, and then click Exit.
