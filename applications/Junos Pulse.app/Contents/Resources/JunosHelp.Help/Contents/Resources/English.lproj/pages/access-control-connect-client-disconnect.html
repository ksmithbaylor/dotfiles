Disconnect an Active Connection

To disconnect from a network:

Option 1

  1. In the Pulse window, click the connection you want to edit to select it.
  2. On the menu bar, click File > Connections > Disconnect.

Option 2

  1. On the Junos Pulse Connections pane, click Disconnect for the connection you want to disconnect.

Option 3

  1. On the menu bar, click the Pulse icon.
  2. Click the connection, and then click Disconnect.
